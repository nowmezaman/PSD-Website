"NEVER JUDGE A BOOK BY ITS COVER", CHRONICLES THE PERSONAL STRUGGLES OF MOTIVATIONAL SPEAKER AND AUTHOR. KALPNASUTHAR, AS SHE GROWS FROM THE GIRL PEOPLE EXPECTED HER TO BE INTO THE WOMAN SHE WAS DESTINED TO BECOME THIS AUTOBIOGRAPHICAL JOURNEY BEGINS WITH A YOUNG KALPNA, A GIRL FROM EAST LONDON, TRYING TO DISCOVER HER IDENTITY IN A WORLD THAT TAKES HER FOR FACE VALUE NEVER UNDERSTANDING WHERE SHE TRULY FITS IN, EACH RAW AND VISCERAL CHAPTER EXPOSES THE SHY MOMENTS AND UNFORTUNATE GROWING PAINS THAT WE ALL EXPERIENCE AS THOUGH KALPNA IS RELIVING THE MOMENTS ON A MOVIE SCREEN, SHE PROVIDES HER AUDIENCE WITH THE NOTES TO LEARN FROM HER MISSTEPS.

IN HER LOWEST MOMENT, SPARKED BY A PAINFUL BREAKUP FROM AN ABUSIVE PARTNER, ALPNA LEARNS THE MOST ABOUT HERSELF AND CLAIMS HER INDEPENDENCE. THE UNDERLYING LESSON SHOWN THROUGHOUT KALPNA'S STORY, IS THAT ONLY ONCE WE ACCEPT WHAT HAS HAPPENED IN OUR PAST, CAN WE FINALLY TAKE OWNERSHIP OF OUR LIFE AND DESIGN OUR FUTURE.
