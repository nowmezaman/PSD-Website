About Kalpna Suthar

About Kalpna Suthar

With the launch of her book In toronto of april of 2016, attendees including bob proctor,Dr demartini mark mckoy and raymond aaron, kalpna suthar has trust herself on the international stage and to the top of everyone's to read list. Being honoured with both a place in the royal library and earning 'the authority in inspiration journey to success' award kalpna has only just begun with her experience in the world of life coaching kalpna has worked with and helped people all over the world. Born and raised in london, kalpna graduated llb (hon) law and criminology at london south back university. She found a love in helping others in need both during and after her studies, most notably devoting her time learning sign language and working with children that are hard of hearing kalpna now travels the world with her mission of sustaining a healthy life. Balanced with care and compassion for everyone she meets.
